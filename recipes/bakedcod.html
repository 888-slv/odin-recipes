Baked Cod

Description

This Mediterranean baked cod is a simple recipe for adding Mediterranean flavor to flaky, healthy, baked cod. Topped with a lemony mix of olives, artichokes, and sun-dried tomatoes, itâ€™s quick and easy enough for the ultimate weeknight dinner, but impressive enough for guests.

Ingredients

  • cod fillets
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Dry fillets and coat with oil
  3. Coat fillets in lemon juice and garlic
  4. Bake for 14 minutes
